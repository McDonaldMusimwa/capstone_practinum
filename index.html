  • "What a great blessing it is to live in this world and have ears with which to hear the direction from a living prophet of God—direction that can bring inner peace in a time of great confusion and difficulty." - Bishop Victor L. Brown

  • "Anchoring our souls to the Lord Jesus Christ requires listening to those He sends." - Elder Neil L. Andersen

In a world inundated with voices, following the prophet offers spiritual safety. Despite potential initial discord, aligning with his teachings leads to increased faith in the Savior. President Russell M. Nelson's call is divine, and his counsel guides us to Christ. Embracing his words with humility and faith ensures our spiritual well-being. As we heed the prophet's voice, we find peace and protection amidst life's turmoil.
